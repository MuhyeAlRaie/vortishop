Feature Focus: Order Management

Streamline your order process from customer to kitchen to delivery

Order Display Screen (ODS)

  • Real-time order tracking from placement to completion
  • Prioritizes tasks for efficient service
  • Reduces errors in order fulfillment
  • Intuitive interface for staff at all levels

Kitchen Display Screen (KDS)

  • Organized view of incoming orders for kitchen staff
  • Tracks preparation times for quality control
  • Ensures correct preparation sequence
  • Synchronizes kitchen workflow with front-of-house

Both systems work together to create a seamless order flow from customer to kitchen to delivery
